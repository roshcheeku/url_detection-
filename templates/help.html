User Manual

Welcome to our web application! This manual will guide you through the steps to log in, navigate through different pages, and use the features available in the application.

Login Page

  1. Open the Application: Navigate to the web application URL in your web browser.
  2. Enter Your Username: Type your desired username in the “Username” field.
  3. Enter Your Email: Type your email address in the “Email” field.
  4. Enter Your Password: Type your password in the “Password” field.
  5. Click “Login”: Press the “Login” button to submit your credentials.
  6. Access Home Page: You will be redirected to the Home Page after a successful login.

Home Page

  1. Toggle Dark Mode: Click the “Toggle Dark Mode” button to switch between light and dark themes.
  2. Submit a URL for Analysis:
    • Enter the URL: Type the URL you wish to analyze in the “Enter URL” field.
    • Submit the URL: Click the “Submit” button (this will be enabled if the URL is valid).
    • View Results: After submission, you will be redirected to the Result Page.
  3. Give Feedback:
    • Click “Give Feedback” Button: This will reveal the feedback form.
    • Enter Feedback: Type your feedback in the “Your Feedback” field.
    • Submit Feedback: Click the “Submit Feedback” button to send your feedback.
  4. Access Other Pages:
    • Profile Page: Click the profile icon to go to the Profile Page.
    • Help Page: Click the “Help” button for help and FAQs.
    • About Us Page: Click the “About Us” button for information about the team and project.

Profile Page

  1. View Profile Information: See your stored email displayed on the page.
  2. View Recent Activity: Check the list of URLs you have submitted under “Recent Activity.”
  3. Return to Home Page: Click the “Home” button to navigate back to the Home Page.

Help Page

  1. Read FAQ: Browse through the frequently asked questions and answers.
  2. Return to Home Page: Click the “Home” button to navigate back to the Home Page.

Result Page

  1. View Analysis Results:
    • Check Accuracy: Review the accuracy of the URL analysis.
    • Check Malicious Status: See if the URL was flagged as malicious.
    • View Graph: Look at the graph (if available) that visualizes the analysis.
  2. Return to Home Page: Click the “Home” button to navigate back to the Home Page.

About Us Page

  1. Read Information: Read about the team and the project details.
  2. Return to Home Page: Click the “Home” button to navigate back to the Home Page.

Troubleshooting and Tips

  • Login Issues: Ensure your username, email, and password are correct.
  • URL Submission: Make sure the URL is valid and includes http:// or https://.
  • Feedback Form: Ensure that feedback is entered before submission.
  • Dark Mode: Toggle dark mode for better visibility or personal preference.
